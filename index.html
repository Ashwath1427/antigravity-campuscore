About Us

What is CampusCore?

Your All-in-One School Management Solution

CampusCore is a robust, custom-built digital platform designed specifically for Delhi Public School Nadergul. It revolutionizes the way our school manages its day-to-day academic and administrative operations.

By bringing attendance tracking, homework management, exam scheduling, and report card generation under one unified ecosystem, CampusCore bridges the communication gap between educators, students, and parents.

Workflow

How Campus Core Works

Seamlessly connecting operations across all departments

Attendance Flow

Teachers mark attendance directly block-by-block. Coordinators and Vice Principal Suman review anomalies, while parents instantly view their child's presence daily.

Marks & Results

Teachers upload exam marks via secure portals. After Coordinator validation, detailed report cards are generated for parents and students to access securely.

Announcements

The Principal or VP can publish school-wide or class-specific notices. Relevant users (Parents, Teachers) get instantly notified on their dashboard modules.

Fee Processing

Management oversees the master fee records and structures. Parents log in to track pending dues, view payment histories, and understand fee breakdowns.

Dynamic Timetable

School leadership and Coordinators draft the master schedules. Teachers view their class assignments, and parents/students check the daily class subjects.

Advanced Reports

High-level statistics spanning across academics and finance are accessible by the Principal and VP to monitor institution performance at a glance.

Homework Tracking

Teachers attach daily classwork/homework tasks. Parents and students utilize this dashboard to keep track of upcoming submissions and track academic rigor.
